Hiya, nice to meet. I’m a UXer and Digital Designer in the Princeton, NJ area. I got my start in architecture and am fascinated by all of the unique spaces around us. Professionally I’ve shifted from 3D modeling and game design to UX. It's felt natural to care for a person's experience in the digital world as an architect does in the physical. My strengths span the end-to-end creation of a product — early-on iterative ideation, advanced prototyping and study design, specs and design system integrations for development, and the creation of final assets like animations, data visualizations, and illustrations.
